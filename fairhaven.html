About this park

A beautiful community park tucked into the wooded hills near Bellingham's Historic Fairhaven Village. The spray park is one of the best in Bellingham. Natural forest shade keeps it cool even on hot days. After the park, walk to Fairhaven Village for ice cream — a perfect family afternoon.

What makes it special
Right next to Historic Fairhaven Village — combine with ice cream or lunch at the village shops.
Tips from families
  • The spray park is fantastic — bring a change of clothes
  • Fairhaven Village is a 5-minute walk — great for lunch after
  • The wooded trails behind the park are beautiful for a stroll
