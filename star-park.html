About this park

Purpose-built specifically for kids, Star Park's 12,000 square foot playground is split into two clearly defined zones — one for ages 0–5 and one for ages 5–12. This thoughtful design means toddlers and big kids both get equipment sized just right for them. The best toddler playground in Ferndale by a mile.

What makes it special
Split into two age-specific zones — 0-5 and 5-12. Rare and extremely thoughtful design.
Tips from families
  • The 0–5 zone has baby swings — confirmed!
  • Limited shade so go in the morning or evening in summer
  • Combine with a visit to downtown Ferndale nearby
