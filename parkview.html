Parkview School Playground

📍 3030 Cornwall Ave, Bellingham, WA 98225 · (360) 676-6433
🌳 About this park

One of the most impressive school playgrounds in Bellingham — Parkview Elementary's playground is a brand new, extensively equipped space that feels more like a premium adventure park than a typical school yard. There's a massive multi-level climbing structure, a rope pyramid, a spectacular wide racing slide, a spiral tube slide, overhead monkey bar traverse, accessible ramp up to the main structure, outdoor musical instruments (xylophone, wind chimes, interactive game panels), balance and stepping equipment, a rock climbing wall, and spring riders. The rubber safety surface throughout makes it very soft underfoot. It's a school playground so access is limited — open after school hours weekdays (around 4pm onward), all day weekends, holidays and summer.

✨ What makes it special
The sheer variety and quality of equipment is unlike any other playground in Bellingham — a rope pyramid, a wide racing slide, outdoor musical instruments, monkey bars, a rock climbing wall, and a huge multi-level accessible climbing structure all in one spot. Brand new rubber safety surface throughout. Cornwall Park with restrooms is just down the hill.
⭐ 5-year-old review

💡 Tips from families
  • This is a school playground — open weekdays after ~4pm, and all day weekends, holidays & summer
  • No restrooms on site — Cornwall Park is right down the hill and has clean restrooms
  • No shade at all — bring sunscreen, hats, and water especially in summer
  • The outdoor musical instruments are a huge hit with younger kids — xylophone, wind chimes, interactive panels
  • The rope pyramid and racing slide are the standout features for older kids
  • The accessible ramp makes the main structure reachable for all kids
  • No swings here — head to Cornwall Park if swings are a must
  • Dogs are welcome in the surrounding area — Cornwall Park nearby has great off-leash space
