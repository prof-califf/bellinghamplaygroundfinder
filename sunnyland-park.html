Sunnyland Park
A small, sunny neighborhood park with a great climbing setup ☀️
🧗 Climbing 🐒 Monkey bars 🤸 Kid swings 🛝 Slide ☀️ Sun 🍺 Brewery nearby
📍 2338 Humboldt St, Bellingham, WA 98225
🌳 About this park

A small but mighty neighborhood park in the heart of Sunnyland. The playground is geared toward bigger kids — ages roughly 5–12 — with a tall monkey bar setup, a rock-style climbing structure with lots to scramble on, two regular swings, one slide, and some fun accessories like a spinning running thing the kids love. There's a green space next to the playground that's great for dogs to run around, and picnic tables for snack breaks. No baby swings, no shade, and no fence, so it's better suited to kids who can be trusted to stay near the play area. Wood chip surface throughout. The big bonus: it's directly across the street from Stemma West Brewery, with several other Bellingham favorites (Otherlands, El Sueñito) just blocks away.

✨ What makes it special
A super fun climbing setup for bigger kids — the rock-style climbing structure plus tall monkey bars give kids a real challenge. Plus the unique "spinning running thing" accessory that kids absolutely love. And let's be honest: the brewery proximity is a real perk for parents.

💡 Tips from families
  • Best for bigger kids (~5–12) — the climbing structure and tall monkey bars are not toddler-friendly
  • Don't miss the spinning running thing — it's the kid favorite
  • No shade and no fence — bring sunscreen, hats, and water in summer
  • No restrooms on site — Stemma West Brewery is right across the street
  • Surrounding green space is great for dogs to run around
  • Pair a playground visit with a beer flight at Stemma, Otherlands, or El Sueñito 🍺
  • Street parking only — usually easy to find
  • Picnic tables on site for snacks or lunch
